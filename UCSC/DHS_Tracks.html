Data associated with the manuscript titled:
"Index and biological spectrum of accessible DNA elements in the human genome"
Meuleman et al.

1. DHS Index
An index of DNase I Hypersensitive Sites (DHSs) in the human genome,
Elements were delineated across 733 biosamples and annotated (Figure panel a).
Each DHS is associated with a confidence score based on mean DNase I signal strength across accessible biosamples.

Scores in the manuscript and original data range from 0 to ~2 (95th percentile).
However, to be compatible with the UCSC browser, scores are scaled to a maximum of 1000 and rounded to the nearest integer.
Therefore, when choosing a score threshold at the top of this page, keep in mind that a score of e.g. 500 corresponds to an original score of 1 (Figure panel b).

2. DHS Vocabulary
A simple vocabulary capturing complex cross-dataset patterning of DHSs.
This vocabulary was obtained by decomposing DHS patterns across 733 datasets into 16 components using Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF).
Each DHS is colored according to its dominant component (Figure panel c).
